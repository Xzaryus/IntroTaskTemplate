How to Work with Me

Here are some tips for working with me effectively:

  • Be clear and concise in your communication.
  • If possible, have some sort of written account of a task that I can refer to.
  • I like to have freedom in the way that I work so I can find my own solutions to problems.
  • I appreciate constructive feedback and open criticism on my work to help me improve.
