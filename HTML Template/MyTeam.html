Software Development Team Roles and Responsibilities

Explain the key roles and responsibilities within your software development team. Use the examples below if applicable, or add your own.

My Role

I am a software engineer, I was meant to be learning under the guidance of the senior engineer of the company but after a couple weeks of my employment she left the company. I am now responsible for maintaining and improving the pre existing software whilst also planning and implementing new features. In the near future, I aim to create entire applications for company use from scratch.

IT manager

The IT manager at my company is called Terry, he is responsible for overseeing all IT operations and ensuring that the technology infrastructure supports the organization's goals. The IT department in my company is quite small so the manager has to be able to handle multiple responsibilities. He is able to solve problems ranging from hardware issues to software conflicts and has knowledge in both areas. He has taught me a lot about the way the company works from observing his approach to problem-solving and decision-making and he had directly assisted me in developing my understanding of the pre existing systems.

Software Tester

The testing of software in my company is not allocated to a specific person, the software is tested by the developers as they go and before deployment. Whenever issues are found whether before or after deployment, they are documented and addressed.

IT Administrator

The IT administrator at my company is called Edgars, he works closely with Terry to ensure that all of the main IT systems are running correctly and whenever issues arise, he is the first point of contact for resolving them. Whenever there are hardware issues or network problems, he is the one to contact for assistance. In the case of a software issue he will explain the problem to me and we will work together to resolve it.

Business Analyst (BA)

The business analysis is done mainly by the operations team who will then arrange meetings with stakeholders to gather and analyze requirements. The results of these meetings are then presented to the IT department where the technical aspects of the requirements are discussed and plans are made.

UX Designer

The main UI/UX designer in my company is called Qaisar, he is responsible for maintaining the many storefront designs and ensuring a good user experience. The storefronts are created by a third party vendor, this means that the he has to work closely with the vendor to ensure that the designs are implemented correctly. He is teaching me how to use the design tools given to us by the vendor and how to change and update the things that we have access to and how to communicate effectively with the them when something needs to be changed that we can't do ourselves.
